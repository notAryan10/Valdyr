# Indian Real Estate Market Trends

Residential sales in India went up around 30% in 2024, mostly driven by tier-1 cities.
Mumbai, Delhi and Bangalore are still the top performers when it comes to price growth.

The affordable housing segment (properties under 45 lakhs) has the most demand right now.
Luxury segment is also picking up, growing roughly 15% year over year.

Because of remote work, cities like Pune, Jaipur and Chandigarh are seeing more buyers now.
Metro city prices vary a lot - anywhere from 5000 to 25000 per sq ft depending on location.

Home loan rates are sitting at about 8.5-9.5% currently.
NRI investments went up by 20% in 2024 which is pushing prices higher in some areas.
Commercial real estate is doing well too with 85%+ occupancy in major cities.
